# Services

Thompson Valley Disposal provides end-to-end disposal and site services across Kamloops and the Thompson Valley. Every project starts with a clear quote and a plan tailored to your property.

## Bin Rentals

Roll-off bins in a range of sizes for residential, commercial, and construction waste. We deliver across Kamloops and the Thompson Valley with flexible rental periods.

- Mini bins for small clean-outs
- Small and medium bins for renovations and roofing
- Large bins for construction and estate clean-outs
- Roll-off bins for commercial and demolition projects

## Excavation

Professional excavation services for residential, commercial, and acreage sites. We safely move earth, rock, and debris to prepare your land for the next phase.

- Site preparation and clearing
- Trenching for utilities and drainage
- Grading for driveways, roads, and landscaping
- Earthmoving and land shaping

## Landscaping

Transform rough or unfinished land into attractive, functional outdoor spaces. We grade, shape, and finish sites to support healthy lawns, gardens, and hardscapes.

- Rough and finish grading
- Soil amendment and prep
- Rock and mulch placement
- Sod and seed bed preparation

## Property Clean-Up

Clear brush, debris, old structures, and yard waste from residential and acreage properties.

- Brush and branch removal
- Fence and shed demolition
- Yard waste hauling
- Post-storm clean-up

## Firewood Sales

Seasoned firewood delivered to your door for wood stoves, fireplaces, and outdoor fires.

- Seasoned hardwood and softwood
- Delivery across the Kamloops area
- Split and ready to burn
- Flexible order sizes

## Recycling

Responsible recycling and diversion of metal, cardboard, concrete, asphalt, and other recyclable materials.

- Construction material diversion
- Metal and cardboard recycling
- Concrete and asphalt recycling
- Compost and organic diversion

## Asbestos Transportation

Licensed transport of asbestos-containing materials with safe handling and proper disposal documentation.

- Licensed and insured transport
- Safe containment and handling
- Proper disposal documentation
- Compliance with BC regulations

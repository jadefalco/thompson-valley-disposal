From Start to Finish

A Clean Property, Delivered

Great property clean-ups start with the right plan. Thompson Valley Disposal assesses your site, brings the right equipment, removes debris, and leaves the land graded and tidy. We also deliver seasoned firewood so you’re ready for winter.

Our Process

  1. Site assessment — We evaluate the scope, access, and materials to remove.
  2. Quote & schedule — You receive a clear plan and straightforward pricing.
  3. Clean-up & removal — Our crew clears debris, brush, and unwanted material.
  4. Grading & finishing — We level and clean the site so it’s ready for use.
  5. Disposal & diversion — Waste is sorted for landfill, recycling, or compost where possible.
  6. Final walkthrough — We confirm the site meets your expectations.

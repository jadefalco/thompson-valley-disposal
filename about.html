Who We Are

Your Local Kamloops Disposal & Site Service Partner

Thompson Valley Disposal is a locally operated disposal and site services company based in Kamloops, BC. We provide roll-off bin rentals, excavation, landscaping, property clean-ups, firewood sales, recycling, and asbestos transportation for homeowners, contractors, businesses, and acreage owners throughout the Thompson Valley.

Founded on a commitment to dependable service and honest pricing, we’ve grown by helping our neighbors manage waste, prepare sites, and keep properties clean. From a single bin for a garage clean-out to a full excavation and clean-up crew, our team brings the equipment and work ethic to get it done right.

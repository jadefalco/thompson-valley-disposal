What We Excavate

Excavation Capabilities

Site Preparation

Clear and prepare your Kamloops property for construction, landscaping, or development with professional site prep services.

  • Land clearing and brush removal
  • Demolition of small structures
  • Grubbing and topsoil stripping
  • Subgrade and base preparation

Trenching

Accurate trenching for utilities, drainage, irrigation, and foundations across Kamloops residential and commercial sites.

  • Utility and service trenches
  • Drainage and French drain trenches
  • Foundation and footing excavation
  • Septic and drainage field prep

Grading

Proper grading directs water away from structures and creates a solid foundation for landscaping, driveways, and buildings.

  • Rough grading for new sites
  • Finish grading for landscaping
  • Driveway and road grading
  • Drainage swales and slope shaping

Earthmoving

Large-scale earthmoving for acreages, rural properties, and commercial sites that require significant cut, fill, or reshaping.

  • Cut and fill operations
  • Pond and basin excavation
  • Road and driveway base work
  • Berm construction and land forming

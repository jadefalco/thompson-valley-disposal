# About Thompson Valley Disposal

Thompson Valley Disposal is a locally operated disposal and site services company based in Kamloops, BC. We provide roll-off bin rentals, excavation, landscaping, property clean-ups, firewood sales, recycling, and asbestos transportation for homeowners, contractors, businesses, and acreage owners throughout the Thompson Valley.

## Our Story

Founded on a commitment to dependable service and honest pricing, Thompson Valley Disposal has grown by helping our neighbors manage waste, prepare sites, and keep properties clean. From a single bin for a garage clean-out to a full excavation and clean-up crew, our team brings the equipment and work ethic to get it done right.

## What We Believe

- **Integrity on every job.** Honest quotes, clear communication, and no hidden fees.
- **Quality that lasts.** We don’t just drop bins — we leave sites graded, cleaned, and ready for what’s next.
- **Local expertise.** We understand Thompson Valley regulations, landfill requirements, and seasonal project demands.
- **Respect for your property.** We treat every site like our own, protecting driveways, lawns, and landscaping.

## Who We Serve

- Homeowners tackling renovations, clean-outs, and yard projects
- Contractors and builders needing reliable bins and site prep
- Businesses and acreage owners requiring ongoing waste management and large-scale clean-ups

## Mission

To provide dependable disposal and site services that help Kamloops and Thompson Valley property owners keep their projects moving and their land clean.
